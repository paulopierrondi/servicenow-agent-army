## Pre-publication checklist (10 items)

1. Hook is one line, both versions, contrarian or numeric.
2. EN version character count is between 800 and 1300.
3. PT-BR version character count is between 800 and 1300.
4. Zero banned words: ensure, crucial, vital, journey, embark, unleash, dive, delve, plethora, indulge, unlock, unveil, elevate, landscape, navigate, daunting, game changer, stand out, unique blend, enhancing, just.
5. Every metric traces to a gallery case file, the Now Assist playbook, or the Pierrondi EA brief. No invented numbers.
6. No client name. Archetypes only: "tier-1 LATAM bank", "global manufacturer LATAM", "Brazilian retail chain".
7. Repo link `github.com/paulopierrondi/servicenow-agent-army` present at end of EN and PT-BR.
8. Max 3 hashtags, all from approved list (#ServiceNow #NowAssist #AIAgents #SADA #FSI #PT-BR).
9. CTA is a specific question, not "thoughts?" or "what do you think?".
10. No emojis. Max 2 Unicode mathematical bold characters per post.

## Engagement triggers

Specific questions that pull comments and DM. Use one per post in the CTA, not the same one twice in the same week.

### Architecture and value

- Qual ultima proposta de arquitetura tinha um numero anexado?
- When was the last value claim you signed off on without a source?
- Show me a 90-day outcome metric for your top roadmap item.
- Who owns it? "The team" is not an answer.
- What is the cost-of-credits estimate for your top Now Assist use case?

### Refusal and pushback

- Where did you say no to AI in the last 30 days?
- Show me a case where AI on top of a broken platform produced ROI.
- What did your last architecture review refuse to draw?
- Quem na sua organizacao tem permissao para dizer "esse caso nao precisa de AI"?

### Gallery and try-it-yourself

- Open the repo, run Pierrondi EA against your worst backlog item. What does it refuse?
- Pick one of the 10 gallery cases. Which one matches your current quarter?
- Replace one Now Assist surface in your roadmap with platform-native. Defend.
- Roda gallery case 08 (performance ACL chain) no seu sub-prod. O que voce acha?

### SADA and governance

- Three alternatives or one? Which architecture decision in your last quarter had three?
- Anti-pattern audit: which of the 20 anti-patterns in the repo are live in your prod today?
- Quem revisa Now Assist Guardian na sua plataforma? Com que cadencia?

### Counter-position

- Discorda? Mostra um caso onde AI sobre dado ruim deu certo.
- Convince me the council should always recommend Now Assist.
- Why is "the team owns it" not group ownership?

## Source-link conventions
